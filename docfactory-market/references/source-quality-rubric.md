# Source Quality Rubric (quick)

Prefer:

- Official app store listings (pricing, screenshots, descriptions)
- Official websites / docs
- Reputable press (major outlets) for trend signals
- Primary research reports (if accessible)
- Direct user reviews (App Store / Play Store)

Be cautious:

- Random blog posts with no data
- Social posts without corroboration
- “Download estimates” from unclear sources

Rule:

- If a metric is not verifiable or the source is weak: use [NO_DATA].
