# Search Queries Template

Use these as starting points. Adapt to the specific idea.

## App store discovery

- "<keyword> app" site:apps.apple.com
- "<keyword> app" site:play.google.com
- "<keyword> subscription app"
- "<keyword> paywall" "review"
- "<competitor name> pricing"

## Reviews & complaints

- site:reddit.com "<keyword>" "app" "alternative"
- site:reddit.com "<competitor>" "issue"
- "<competitor>" "refund" review
- "<competitor>" "too expensive" review

## Trend signals

- "<category>" "2025" report
- "<category>" "2026" app trend
- "Google Trends" "<keyword>"
