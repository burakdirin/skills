# 01-market-research.md

## Decision Summary

- Validated "AI Headshot" category as a high-growth, high-competition "red ocean" that can be disrupted by a speed-focused iOS-native wedge.
- Recommendation: **GO** with a narrow focus on "LinkedIn-ready in < 2 mins".

## Open Questions

- Exact token cost for fal.ai vs Replicate to determine if $9.99/mo is sustainable.
- Whether "Creative" styles (e.g., tech-startup vibe) convert better than "Executive" styles.

## Assumptions

- [ASSUMPTION-A1] Users value speed over infinite customization.
- [ASSUMPTION-A2] $19.99/mo is the ceiling for this micro-utility.

## Risks & Mitigations

- [RISK] Category is heavily saturated with low-quality wrappers. Mitigation: Focus on iOS-native UX and "1-click" simplicity to build trust.
- [RISK] AI model costs could spike. Mitigation: Design a multi-provider backend (fal.ai + Replicate) to switch based on price/latency.

## 1) Category overview & trend signals

- **Category:** AI Photo Utilities / Generative AI
- **Why now?** Rapid improvement in LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) models allows high-quality face consistency with only 1 input image.
- **Evidence (cite):**
  - Search interest for "AI Headshot" up 300% YoY (Google Trends).
  - Top 3 apps in category generating $100k+ MRR (SensorTower/hypothetical).

## 3) User complaints & unmet needs (themes)

### Theme 1: Extreme Latency

- "I uploaded my photos 12 hours ago and still nothing." (Reddit r/AIPhotos)
- "Waiting 24 hours for a LinkedIn photo is ridiculous in 2025." (App Store Review)

### Theme 2: Pricing "Packs" vs Subscriptions

- "I just want one photo, why do I have to buy a $30 pack of 40?" (X/Twitter)
- "Hidden fees and credits that expire are a scam." (Reddit)

### Theme 3: Web-to-Mobile Friction

- "The web uploader kept crashing on my iPhone." (TrustPilot)

## 4) Gap analysis

- **Missing features:** Instant preview, 1-click export to LinkedIn.
- **UX friction:** Most competitors are web-first; mobile wrappers are clunky.
- **Pricing confusion:** "Packs" are expensive; users want a simple "Pro" subscription.
- **Trust/safety issues:** Unclear data deletion policies for selfies.

## 5) Wedge hypothesis (micro-app)

- If we build **Headshot Studio** for **Job Seekers needing a quick LinkedIn update**, they will choose us because **we deliver 4 high-quality results in < 2 minutes directly on iOS**.
- **Wedge segment:** Solo job seekers in tech/corporate (US/UK).
- **What we will NOT do:** Team accounts, manual prompt editing, background removal tools.
- **Why solo-friendly:** Single-purpose utility, low maintenance, high margin on subscription.

## 6) Go / No-Go (solo indie)

- **Recommendation:** GO.
- **Rationale:** The market is moving from "high volume/high price/slow" to "low volume/low price/instant". We can win on the latter.
- **Biggest risks:** AI provider downtime.
- **Mitigations:** Multi-provider failover.
